Get Scootin

Locate with app

Use the app to find the nearest scooter to you. We are continously placing scooters in the areas with most demand, so one should never be too far away.

Pick your Scooter

We show the most important info to the scooters closest to you. So you know how much charge they have left and can see how much it will cost.

Enjoy the ride

Scan the QR code on the bike will unlock. Retract the cable lock, and put a helmet on, and you are off. Always lock bikes away from walkways and accessibility ramps.

Easy to use riding telemetry

The Scoot app is available with riding telemetry. This means it can show you your average speed, how long you've been using the scooter, your traveling distance, and many more things all in an easy to use app.
